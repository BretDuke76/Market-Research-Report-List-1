<p><strong>The Solar Home Lighting Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Off-grid Solar</li><li>Grid-tied Solar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solar home lighting equipment market comprises two main types: off-grid solar and grid-tied solar. Off-grid solar systems operate independently of the electrical grid, making them ideal for remote areas where grid access is unavailable. These systems include solar panels, batteries, and inverters to store and manage energy. In contrast, grid-tied solar systems connect to the electrical grid, allowing homeowners to use solar energy while remaining connected to utility services, often facilitating net metering for excess energy production.</p></p>
